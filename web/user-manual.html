Getting Started

To start using KHOJ, simply visit our website at www.khoj.com. You can begin searching for businesses immediately without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to leave reviews, save favorite businesses, and receive personalized recommendations.

Searching for Businesses

Use the search bar on the homepage to find businesses. Enter keywords related to the type of business or service you're looking for (e.g., "Italian restaurant", "plumber", "hair salon") and your location (e.g., "downtown", "near me", "zip code").

Viewing Business Profiles

Click on a business from the search results to view its profile. The profile includes detailed information such as business hours, address, contact information, customer reviews, photos, and a map showing the location.

Using Filters and Sorting

After performing a search, you can refine your results using filters and sorting options. Filters allow you to narrow down businesses based on criteria like price range, ratings, and specific services offered. Sorting options let you arrange results by relevance, distance, rating, or popularity.

Leaving Reviews

To leave a review for a business, you must be logged into your KHOJ account. Navigate to the business profile and click the "Write a Review" button. Provide a rating (1-5 stars) and write a detailed review of your experience. Be honest and respectful in your feedback.

Contacting Businesses

You can contact businesses directly through KHOJ. Business profiles often include contact information such as phone numbers and email addresses. Some businesses may also have a "Contact" button that allows you to send a message directly through the KHOJ platform.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues while using KHOJ, please visit our Help Center or contact our support team. Common issues and their solutions are often available in the FAQ section. For personalized assistance, you can submit a support ticket or use the live chat feature (if available).
