Discover the World Around You with KHOJ

KHOJ is a local search engine that helps you find what you're looking for, right in your neighborhood. Explore local businesses, events, and more with ease.

Overview

KHOJ is designed to provide you with the most relevant and up-to-date information about your local area. Whether you're searching for a new restaurant, a nearby event, or a specific service, KHOJ delivers results that are tailored to your location.

Features

Explore the Power of KHOJ

KHOJ offers a range of features designed to enhance your local search experience.

Local Business Search

Find businesses in your area with detailed information, including hours, contact details, and customer reviews.

Nearby Events

Discover upcoming events, concerts, and festivals happening near you.

Real-Time Updates

Get the latest information on traffic, weather, and other real-time updates affecting your local area.

User Reviews

Read reviews from other users to make informed decisions about local businesses and services.
